Bailiff Services

Tesa Debt Recovery Management Limited provides professional private bailiff services and court-certified bailiff services to businesses and individuals seeking to recover overdue payments or enforce court judgments. As a dedicated private bailiff service provider, we do not offer government or municipal bailiff services but focus solely on private debt recovery and enforcement.

Our services also include levying distress for business properties, assisting landlords and commercial property owners in recovering unpaid rent through legal distress procedures. With years of expertise, we offer reliable and efficient assistance in executing court orders and dealing with debtors who refuse to settle their debts.

How We Help

Our team of skilled court-certified bailiffs specializes in debt enforcement, ensuring that your rights are upheld and your debts are recovered efficiently. We handle the entire process with professionalism and discretion, allowing you to focus on your business while we take care of the recovery process.

Types of Bailiff Services We Offer:

  • Rent Arrears Collection: Assisting landlords in recovering unpaid rent and resolving tenant disputes.
  • Seizure of Assets: Recovering assets through lawful seizure in accordance with court decisions.
  • Eviction Services: Ensuring a legal and smooth eviction process for landlords needing to remove tenants.
  • Repossession of Goods: Taking legal action to repossess goods that have not been paid for.

At Tesa, we work closely with clients to understand their specific needs and offer tailored bailiff services that meet the requirements of the case. Our legal team ensures full compliance with relevant laws and regulations to guarantee the recovery process is seamless and hassle-free.
